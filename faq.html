Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our tutoring services? Find answers to the most common questions below.

← Back to Tutors

How much does tutoring cost?

Our tutoring services are completely free! As a student-run club, we provide peer tutoring to help fellow students succeed.

How do I book a tutoring session?

On our website, press the "Request a Tutor" button. Include your name/grade, subject, your email, and your requested tutor. They will reach out to you personally.

Where do tutoring sessions take place?

Sessions typically happen during lunch periods in designated areas at Waterdown District High School, like our library and resource room.

How long are tutoring sessions?

Sessions are usually 20-75 minutes, depending on the tutor's availability and your needs. This can be discussed when you book.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Please give at least 12 hours notice if you need to cancel. Let your tutor know as soon as possible so they can help other students.

Can I get help with homework or test preparation?

Absolutely! Our tutors can help with homework, concept review, test preparation, and general subject support.

What if my tutor doesn't respond to my email?

Tutors typically respond within 24-48 hours. If you don't hear back, feel free to contact the club directly at WDHSPOKtutoring@gmail.com.

Can I request a specific tutor?

Yes! You can choose any tutor from our list based on their subject expertise and availability that works for you.

Do you offer group tutoring?

Some tutors may be available for small group sessions. Ask them directly when you email to inquire about group options.

How do I become a tutor?

We're always looking for dedicated students to join our team! Contact the club at WDHSPOKtutoring@gmail.com to express your interest.
